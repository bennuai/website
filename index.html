Global Waste to Grow by 70 Percent by 2050

Without urgent action, global annual waste generation is expected to jump to 3.4 billion tonnes over the next 30 years, up from 2.01 billion tonnes in 2016. Parallely waste management cost has increased by 270% over the last decade.

We have a solution

50% of waste could be recycled

We sort your waste automatically

50% of trash in the general waste could be recycled, while 25% of trash in the recycling bins is contaminated with unrecycable waste. We present Janus a bin that sorts trash automatically. Just throw your waste away, and let us take care of the rest, while saving over 20% of your waste managment cost.

Our values

Our values and goals are what make us who we are

  • Values

Be sustainable

We are committed to incorporate recyclable materials and renewable energy from local sources whenever possible. We actively consider the environment in the choices we make and work towards a more circular economy. We strive for passionate collaborators, who are dedicated to act as environmental advocates and to improve the health and happiness of the world.

Be customer-centric

We take the engagement of customers very serious. We want to build customer relations by creating awareness and responsible choices that respect the earth and the environment. In partnering with our customers early on and putting ourselves in their shoes, we can understand their needs and thus deliver the best experience. In doing so we are always open to challenge our ideas of what’s possible.
